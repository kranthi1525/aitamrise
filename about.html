About Us

About AITAM RISE

AITAM RISE FOUNDATION is the official incubation center of Aditya Institute of Technology and Management, Tekkali. Established as a Section 8 Company, the foundation operates as an incubator dedicated to nurturing innovative ideas and empowering entrepreneurs. It provides structured support for startups, driving innovation, enabling commercialization, and creating lasting economic impact.

Our Vision

To be a pioneering incubator that empowers innovators and entrepreneurs to drive sustainable rural development, inclusive economic growth, and long-term social impact

Our Mission

  • Provide strategic mentorship and guidance
  • Connect startups with funding opportunities
  • Foster innovation through collaboration
  • Build sustainable business models

Our Governance Council

A diverse council of leaders ensures strategic direction, accountability, and sustainable growth for AITAM RISE’s incubation initiatives.
